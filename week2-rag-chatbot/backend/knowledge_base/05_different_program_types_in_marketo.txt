Different Program Types in Adobe Marketo Engage
Overview
Adobe Marketo Engage has four official program types: Event, Engagement, Email, and Default. Programs act as containers for local assets such as landing pages, emails, and Smart Campaigns.
Event Programs
Event programs are used for campaigns tied to a specific time and date, including live or online events. They help coordinate, facilitate, and track event-related marketing activities.
Engagement Programs
Engagement programs are designed for drip nurturing. They support ongoing communication at a set periodic schedule, which makes them suitable for lead nurture or onboarding tracks.
Email Programs
Email programs are built for classic mail blasts. Adobe notes that they include built-in features such as A/B testing and Email Head Start, and they are especially useful for one-off targeted email campaigns.
Default Programs
Default programs are used for campaigns that do not fit the Event, Engagement, or Email categories. Adobe describes them as flexible enough for varied marketing initiatives and even operational use cases such as lifecycle tracking or scoring programs.
