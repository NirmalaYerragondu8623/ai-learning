Smart Campaign in Adobe Marketo Engage
Overview
A Smart Campaign is one of the core automation objects in Marketo.[cite:25] It is structured around three main areas: Smart List, Flow, and Schedule.[cite:25]
Structure
The Smart List defines who qualifies for the campaign.[cite:25] The Flow defines what actions occur for those qualified people, and the Schedule controls when processing happens.[cite:25]
Campaign logic
Smart Campaigns can be batch-driven or trigger-driven.[cite:25] Batch campaigns process people on a defined schedule, while trigger campaigns listen continuously for qualifying behaviors or data changes.[cite:25]
Practical importance
Smart Campaigns are central to Marketo automation because they turn audience rules into executable processes.[cite:25][cite:28] Their flexibility is powerful, but as automation scales, clear governance and reliable data become essential for maintainability.[cite:33][cite:28]
