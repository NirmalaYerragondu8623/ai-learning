Reporting and Analytics in Adobe Marketo Engage and Oracle Eloqua
Oracle Eloqua
Oracle Eloqua provides campaign operational reports directly related to active and recent campaign behavior. Oracle's reporting includes Campaign Audit, Campaign Entry, Campaign Exit, Campaign Email Statistics, Campaign SMS Statistics, and Stuck Records Report.
These reports help marketers track campaign changes, understand how contacts entered or exited the workflow, measure delivery and engagement outcomes, and identify records that are not progressing as expected. Oracle also notes that campaign operational reports cover only the last three months, so longer-term analysis should use Insight reports or dashboards.
Adobe Marketo Engage
In Marketo, reporting and analytics are closely connected to Smart Campaign logic, Smart Lists, and program structure. Because Smart Lists are used in campaigns, reports, segmentations, and engagement programs, audience logic directly influences reporting quality and interpretation.
Marketo operational analysis typically focuses on who qualifies for a Smart Campaign, how the flow processes those people, and whether scheduled or triggered logic is working as intended. This makes reporting highly actionable, but it also means weak database hygiene can reduce both campaign performance and measurement accuracy.
