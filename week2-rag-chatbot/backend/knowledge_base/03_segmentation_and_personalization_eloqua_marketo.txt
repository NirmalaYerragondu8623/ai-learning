Segmentation and Personalization in Oracle Eloqua and Adobe Marketo Engage
Oracle Eloqua
Segmentation in Eloqua is closely linked to campaign audience entry and segment evaluation. The Segment Members element controls who enters the campaign and whether new contacts continue entering while the campaign remains active.
Personalization in Eloqua is often expressed through campaign pathing and asset-level differentiation within a multi-step workflow. Because Eloqua is built for orchestrated journeys, personalization can happen not only in content but also in how different contacts move through different campaign paths.
Adobe Marketo Engage
Segmentation in Marketo is strongly driven by Smart Lists, which Adobe describes as a way to find specific groups of people using simple filters. Smart Lists are used throughout Marketo in Smart Campaigns, programs, reports, segmentations, business models, engagement programs, dynamic content, and more.
Personalization in Marketo benefits from this reusable audience logic because Smart Lists can exist in the Database for shared use or inside programs for local use. This structure supports precise targeting and scalable campaign design, but it depends on accurate criteria and well-maintained person data.
