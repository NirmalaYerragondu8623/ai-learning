Segmentation and Personalization in Oracle Eloqua and Adobe Marketo Engage
Oracle Eloqua
Segmentation in Eloqua is closely linked to campaign audience entry and segment evaluation.[cite:24] The Segment Members element controls who enters the campaign and whether new contacts continue entering while the campaign remains active.[cite:24]
Personalization in Eloqua is often expressed through campaign pathing and asset-level differentiation within a multi-step workflow.[cite:8] Because Eloqua is built for orchestrated journeys, personalization can happen not only in content but also in how different contacts move through different campaign paths.[cite:8][cite:24]
Adobe Marketo Engage
Segmentation in Marketo is strongly driven by Smart Lists, which Adobe describes as a way to find specific groups of people using simple filters.[cite:28] Smart Lists are used throughout Marketo in Smart Campaigns, programs, reports, segmentations, business models, engagement programs, dynamic content, and more.[cite:28]
Personalization in Marketo benefits from this reusable audience logic because Smart Lists can exist in the Database for shared use or inside programs for local use.[cite:28] This structure supports precise targeting and scalable campaign design, but it depends on accurate criteria and well-maintained person data.[cite:28][cite:33]
