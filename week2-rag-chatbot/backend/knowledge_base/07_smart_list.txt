Smart List in Adobe Marketo Engage
Overview
Adobe defines Smart Lists as a way to find specific groups of people using simple filters. They are one of the most widely used targeting mechanisms in Marketo.
Where Smart Lists are used
Smart Lists are used in Smart Campaigns, programs, reports, segmentations, business models, engagement programs, and dynamic content. This broad usage makes them foundational to both campaign execution and operational management in Marketo.
Types of placement
Adobe explains that Smart Lists can be created in two places. They can exist in the Database for shared or global use, or inside programs as local assets relevant only to that program.
Behavior
Smart Lists are rule-based and dynamic. Their membership changes automatically based on whether people meet the defined criteria, which makes them highly useful for ongoing segmentation and automation.
